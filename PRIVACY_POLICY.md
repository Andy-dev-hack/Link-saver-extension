# Privacy Policy for Link Saver

**Last Updated**: January 5, 2026

## Overview

Link Saver is a Chrome Extension that helps you organize and save links locally on your device.

## Data Collection

- **What we collect**: Your saved links and folder names.
- **Where it's stored**: Locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
- **Who has access**: Only you. No data is sent to external servers.
- **No personal information**: We do not collect names, emails, or any personally identifiable information.

## Permissions Explained

Link Saver requests the following permissions:

- **`tabs` and `activeTab`**: To read the current tab's URL and title when you click "Save Tab". This allows the extension to automatically fill in the link information.
- **`storage`**: To save your links and folders locally on your device so they persist between browser sessions.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:

- Send your data to external servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Track your browsing activity
- Use analytics or tracking tools

## Your Rights

You can:

- Delete all your data by uninstalling the extension
- Export your data manually (links are stored in `chrome.storage.local`)
